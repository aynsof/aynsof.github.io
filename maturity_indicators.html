Indicators of Maturity

We are citing a number of different pieces of research about maturity of delivery. These include:

State of DevOps Report

  • Job satisfaction is #1 predictor of profitability, market share, and productivity
  • How long would it take you to release a single line of code into production?
    • Should be less than 1hr - Requires Continuous Delivery
    • “If it hurts, do it more frequently”
  • Experimentation
  • Small batches
  • Feedback loops
  • Outsourcing is a negative indicator

Westrum Organisational Culture

Strong positive correlation between productivity and culture

Google reWork Report

  • Psychological safety is #1 predictor of high performance
  • "Can we take risks without feeling insecure or embarrassed?

Stripe - The Developer Coefficient

  • Access to developers is a bigger threat to success than access to capital
  • Estimated $300bn USD annual loss from developer inefficiency, including:
    • Legacy systems and technical debt
    • Leadership prioritisation
    • Building custom technology

Incidents - Trends from the Trenches

Subbu Allamaraju, Vice President of Technology at Expedia

  • Change safety - progressive deployments (canaries), feature flags, blue/green, predictable rollbacks, shadow testing
  • Fault containment/redundancy - redundant copies (active or passive), compartmentalisation, low coupling
  • Importance of automation, cloud to reduce undifferentiated heavy lifting
  • Observability of systems
    • >
  • Importance of post-incident rituals
